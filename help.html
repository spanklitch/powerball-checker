PowerBall Find Me!

How to Use This App

What This App Does

This app lets you save your PowerBall lottery numbers and automatically checks them against the latest drawing results. It tells you instantly if you've won and how much!

How to Enter Your Numbers

  1. Tap the first white circle to start entering your numbers
  2. Type a 2-digit number (use 01-09 for single digits, like 07)
  3. The cursor automatically moves to the next field after you enter 2 digits
  4. Enter all 5 white ball numbers (1-69) and 1 red PowerBall number (1-26)
  5. When complete, the "Save My Numbers" button will glow - tap it!
Tip: Your numbers are saved on your device. They'll still be there when you close and reopen the app!

Understanding Results

After saving your numbers, the app compares them to the latest PowerBall drawing:

  • Matching numbers glow gold in the "Last Drawing" section
  • Green "Congrats" message = You won something!
  • Orange "Try Again" message = No matches this time

Install on iPhone (Add to Home Screen)

  1. Open this app in Safari (not Chrome)
  2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" in the top right corner
  5. The app icon will appear on your home screen!
Why Safari? Only Safari supports adding web apps to your iPhone home screen. Chrome and other browsers don't have this feature on iOS.

Install on Android

  1. Open this app in Chrome
  2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top right
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
  4. Tap "Add" to confirm

About Notifications

This app doesn't send push notifications. Instead:

  • Open the app after each PowerBall drawing (Mon, Wed, Sat nights)
  • It automatically fetches the latest results and checks your numbers
  • Results update within a few hours after each drawing
Drawing Schedule: PowerBall drawings are held Monday, Wednesday, and Saturday at 10:59 PM ET.

Troubleshooting

App shows old results?

  • Pull down to refresh the page
  • Or close and reopen the app
  • Results may take a few hours to update after a drawing

Numbers not saving?

  • Make sure all 6 numbers are entered
  • White balls must be unique (no duplicates)
  • White balls: 1-69, PowerBall: 1-26
